Credits

Rakushki was created by the Oswego CSC380 team, IRONKLADD. KLADD is an abbreviation for the team's members: Kenny Roffo, Hsueh Kuan Lu, Alexander Jansing, Dean Tupper, and Daniel Wysocki.
